Bibliographic Information

Jardín o tiempo

by Alejandra Pizarnik

Original Source

The facsimile files were provided by Prof. Daniel E. Balderston for teaching purposes. Their transcription was made by Helena Bermúdez for the sole purpose of being used during this workshop.

Witness List

  • Witness one: La sombra de la imagen

Electronic Edition Information:

Publication Details:

Work not for publication. Developed by Helena Bermúdez and David Birnbaum to be used during the conference Text as Process: Genetic and Textual Criticism in the Digital Age (Pittsburgh, April 4-6).

Encoding Principles

Codification done using the parallel segmentation method. Only the lines were aligned (no tokenization, no encoding of common text).
